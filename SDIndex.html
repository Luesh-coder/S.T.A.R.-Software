S.T.A.R.

Spotlight Tracking with Automated Recognition

Project Overview

The Spotlight Tracking With Automated Recognition (S.T.A.R.) project is an automated spotlight system designed to track and illuminate performers on stage without the need for manual operation or expensive lighting consoles. Traditional stage lighting techniques often require a dedicated spotlight operator or costly moving-head fixtures with complex control systems. These approaches increase labor, equipment, and programming costs while limiting accessibility for smaller venues, schools, and theaters. S.T.A.R. seeks to provide a cost-effective, compact, and user-friendly alternative.
